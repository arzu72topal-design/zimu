Our mission

How it works

Four steps.
Full control.

01
Scan your space

Take a photo of any room. AI detects and catalogs every item — furniture, electronics, clothing, books.

02
Build your inventory

Each item gets a digital product passport with category, condition, value, CO₂ impact, and more.

03
Check before buying

Scan anything in a store. Instantly see if you own it, what it costs new vs. secondhand, and its environmental cost.

04
Sell, swap or gift

List items to your trusted network in one tap. Your circle gets first access, reducing waste and building community.
